Elevate Your Portraits

Portraits capture more than just faces; they tell stories. Here are five essential techniques:

  1. Focus on the Eyes: Sharp eyes create a powerful connection with the viewer.
  2. Play with Lighting: Experiment with natural and artificial light for dynamic effects.
  3. Background Matters: Choose clean or complementary backgrounds to enhance the subject.
  4. Direct Your Subject: Provide guidance for natural and engaging poses.
  5. Use a Wide Aperture: Blur the background and make your subject pop with a shallow depth of field.

Practice these tips to create portraits that truly stand out!
